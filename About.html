ABOUT

My ability to communicate ideas effectively, work as a leadership role, and present unique perspectives allow for a competitive advantage on any collaborative team. Furthermore, my empathic nature and self-starter attitude would thrive in an environment where amazing interactions are practiced.

EXPERIENCE

Instil, 2022 — Brand Marketing Intern

  • Perform market analysis and research on client and competition to then use the data to help position the brand marketing as well as strategize on deliverables for the organization.
  • Collaboration and coffee chats with both the marketing and product teams create space for creative critique as well as trans-disciplinary learning.
  • While reporting directly to Marketing Management, my role also assists in the daily admin, creative, and clerical work (ie; Blog Imagery, Slide/Pitch Decks, and Asset Documentation/Organization).

Snap Inc, 2021 — Snap Design Scholar

  • Selected as 1 of 15 design scholars picked from a competitive pool of designers within the Los Angeles Metropolitan Area.
  • I refined my skills in typography, photo manipulation, and illustration, as well as, skills in team communication and trans-disciplinary collaboration.
  • Acquired new tools that allowed my creative processes to be more efficient and effective in communicating my concepts.

ArtCenter College of Design, 2021 — Design Creative

  • Chosen to work specifically with the Center of Diversity, Equity and Inclusion to create designs that spread across the various DEI social mediums.
  • In addition to the exploration of brand direction I was also selected for the creation of deliverables for DEIs new venture  The Collective.
  • Practice of professional hand over of digital assets as well as trans-discipline communication.

Sole Folks at Leimert Park, 2020 — Graphic Designer II

  • As 2020 seemed to be the year of daily historic events, my cohorts and I collaborated with partners to support and empower Black and minority-owned companies and brands in Los Angeles. The course was structured like a design consultancy, with students providing effective services and deliverables  including brand and product strategy, brand identity, product design and environmental design services.
